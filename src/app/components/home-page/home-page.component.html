JILINDE PORTABLE HEALTH RECORDS

Data Protection

The use of a private, permissioned Hyperledger Fabric blockchain network technology has enhanced privacy and security of patients medical records.

Ownership

One of the main objectives of the system is to place a patient in control of his or her medical records. The patient dictates which health care provider can get which access rights to his or her medical records.

Emergency Aid

The summary medical record provided by the system provides a harmless subset of a patient's medical record open for viewing by any healthcare provider during emergency situations.
